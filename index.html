Career Goals

I want to develop brain-computer interface technologies that provide sensory feedback for virtual reality experiences. VR/MR became big in 2016. However, its growth and adoption into mainstream use has been limited. One of the core problems facing these new platforms is the lack of truly immersive haptic feedback to match the visual experience. We have motion-tracking controllers that afford a higher sense of immersion and interaction with the world, but the experience they offer is still far from feeling realistic. I want to get our brains more involved in the gaming experience. I believe that non-invasive, neurohaptic interfaces will be ideal for providing this experience by utilizing, trans-cranial stimulation to stimulate the somatosensory cortex.
