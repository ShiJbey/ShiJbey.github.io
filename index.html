About Me

I graduated from Carnegie Mellon University in 2019 with a M.S. in Biomedical Engineering, and graduated from the University of Delaware in 2017 with a B.S. in Computer Science and a B.S. in Neuroscience.

In grad school, I conducted research on electroencephalography (EEG)-based brain-computer interfaces for virtual reality.

I currently work in the field of multi-robot simulation and AI. In my freetime, I enjoy learning about new game development techniques/teachnologies. I do my best, to apply what I've learned in side projects.
